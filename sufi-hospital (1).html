What's Covered Under Health Card?

Surgical Procedures General & emergency surgeries covered under the scheme at zero cost to patient
In-Patient Admission Hospital bed, nursing care, and ICU services included during treatment
Medicines & Lab Tests Essential medicines and diagnostic tests covered during your hospital stay
Maternity Services Mother and child healthcare including delivery and postnatal care
